About DASAD

Founded in 2010, DASAD has grown from a boutique financial research firm to a leading consulting partner for enterprises and investors worldwide. Our core mission is to demystify complex financial markets and deliver actionable insights that drive sustainable growth.

Your Trusted Financial Partner

Headquartered in St Albans, England, DASAD specializes in global financial market research, data analysis, risk assessment and strategic consulting. We serve a diverse clientele including multinational corporations, financial institutions, high-net-worth individuals, and emerging startups.

Our team consists of senior financial analysts with average 15+ years of industry experience, certified risk managers, and strategic consultants who have worked with Fortune 500 companies. We adhere to rigorous work standards, combining real-time market data and academic theories to provide objective, data-driven recommendations.

Unlike generic consulting firms, we tailor every solution to your unique business context, risk appetite, and growth objectives. Our transparent pricing model and long-term partnership approach have earned us a 98% client retention rate over the past decade.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive suite of financial consulting services designed to address every stage of your business or investment lifecycle. From market entry strategy to ongoing risk management, our solutions adapt to your evolving needs.

📊

Market Analysis

Real-time tracking of global financial markets (equities, fixed income, commodities, forex) with daily and weekly market reports. Our analysis includes trend forecasting, volatility assessment, and correlation studies to identify emerging opportunities and threats.

📑

Financial Consulting

Tailored financial planning including cash flow management, capital structure optimization, tax-efficient asset allocation, and retirement planning. We work with you to align your financial strategy with your short and long-term goals.

🛡️

Risk Assessment

Comprehensive risk identification across credit, market, operational, and liquidity dimensions. We develop customized risk mitigation strategies and stress-testing frameworks to protect your assets and ensure business continuity.

🔍

Data Research

Advanced financial data collection, cleansing, and predictive analytics using AI-powered tools. Our research covers industry benchmarks, competitor analysis, consumer behavior, and regulatory impact assessment.

🤝

Business Consulting

Strategic guidance for corporate finance decisions including merger & acquisition evaluation, investment project appraisal, capital raising, and corporate governance optimization for SMEs and large enterprises.

💡

Investment Advice

Objective, conflict-free investment recommendations based on your risk tolerance, time horizon, and financial goals. We provide portfolio rebalancing guidance and performance monitoring across asset classes.
